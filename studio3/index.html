Two-Player Hangman & Pig Dice Game

Challenge your friends with Hangman or test your luck in the Pig dice game!

Game Objectives

  • **Hangman**: Player 1 sets the word, and Player 2 guesses it. The starting player alternates with every game refresh.
  • **Pig Dice Game**: Roll the dice to reach 30 points or more. Beware of rolling a "1" or "snake eyes"!
